    THE CLIO CUP


    The CLIO CUP is a single-brand trophy created and operated by Alpine Racing known until 2020 as Renault Sport.
    With over fifty years of experience, the know-how of Renault Sport Racing benefits both professional drivers and gentlemen through a modern and competitive product from 2020: the fifth generation of the New Clio Cup.

    The 2020 was the last season with the Clio 4, with whom we won the Italian championship with Massimiliano Danetti. During the season we were also the team with the most points accumulated. In addition to Danetti, Fulvio Ferri and Paolo Felisa also raced during the season.

    The 2021 was the year when the European was introduced with the new fifth-generation Clio. We participated in the 6 dates of the championship group A (Italian) also part of the European championship. Massimiliano Danetti, Fabrizio Ongaretto, Giacomo Jelmini, Paolo Felisa, Pietro Bacchi and Felice Jelmini raced in the 2021 season.
